About Me

My name is Bernard Williams and for the past 16 years I managed the delivery of software solutions to large organizations.

I am a Program Manager for The Experience Engine where I manage the delivery of ground breaking technology to a large cruise line. Before joining The Experience Engine, I was a Program Managaer with the Justice & Public Safety practice with Microsoft. There, I supported the delivery of complex solutions to public safety organizations. Prior to this, I was the Director of Professional Services with Toptech Systems (an IDEX business unit), Directed the Professional Services for NCR's Travel Self-Service business in North America, and was a Manager of Program Management with Motorola’s Public Safety Software Applications business unit.

Prior to the private sector I served as a Law Enforcement officer in Central Florida and in the United States Marine Corp.
